## Tips for Furniture Parts

### Material Selection

| Application | Recommended | Notes |
|-------------|-------------|-------|
| Drawer slides | PETG, Nylon | Low friction, durable |
| Feet/Levelers | TPU, PETG | Grip and cushioning |
| Handles/Knobs | PETG, ABS | Strength and appearance |
| Brackets | PETG, ABS | Structural strength |
| Shelf supports | PETG | Load bearing |

### Considerations

- Load bearing capacity
- Long-term creep under constant stress
- Aesthetics and finish
- IKEA parts are commonly needed!
